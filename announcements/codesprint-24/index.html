CodeSprint LA 2024 Results

Posted May 21, 2024 ‐ 1 min read

Last Saturday, May 18th, our club participated in CodeSprint LA '24, a 5 hour ICPC-style contest open to teams all around the world.

Congratulations to our team, CCA Conspiracy, which placed 2nd in the high school division, solving 12 out of the 13 problems!
